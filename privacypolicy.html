Our policy

Pedal Faster is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy
and to safeguarding the integrity of your personal data. This policy explains how any personal
data Pedal Faster collects from you, or that you provide to Pedal Faster, will be processed. We hope that
it will answer any questions and allay any fears that you may have about these issues.
For the purpose of the Data Protection Act 2018 (the "Act"), the data controller at Pedal Faster is
The Company Secretary of Pedal Faster limited.

Information Pedal Faster may collect from you

Pedal Faster may collect and process the following data about you:

  • Information that you provide by filling in forms on the website www.PedalFaster.com ("the Site").
    This includes information provided at the time of registering to use the Site, subscribing to
    Pedal Faster’s service, posting material or requesting further services. Pedal Faster may also ask you
    for information when you enter a competition or promotion sponsored by Pedal Faster, and when you
    report a problem with the Site.
  • If you contact Pedal Faster, a record may be kept of that correspondence.
  • Pedal Faster may also ask you to complete surveys that it uses for research purposes, although you
    do not have to respond to them.
  • Details of transactions you carry out through the Site and of the fulfilment of your orders.
    Details relating to your abandoned baskets and incomplete checkouts on the Site.

  • Details of your visits to the Site including, but not limited to, traffic data, location data,
    weblogs and other communication data, whether this is required for Pedal Faster's own billing
    purposes or otherwise and the resources that you access.

  • Publicly available business or authority contact information for legitimate interest purposes.
