About Us

Pedal Faster is a magazine that specializes giving you the newest information in the bicycle world. We can help you to choose a new bicycle, building it especially for you, or then help you to repair your old one. Over the years we have managed to collect enormous knowledge in the field of bicycles and our goal is to offer you the best there is available.
We also try to keep you up to date with events in bicycle world like tournaments and races...
